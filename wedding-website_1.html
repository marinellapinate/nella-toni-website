Directions

How to get There

The wedding is in the town of Altafulla, about 1 hour from Barcelona's airport. Here's how to get there.

Fly into Barcelona

Fly to Josep Tarradellas Barcelona-El Prat Airport. It's the closest international airport, about 1 hour from Altafulla.

Rent a Car Recommended

We strongly encourage renting a car at the airport. There is no Uber and taxis are very limited in town. A car gives you full freedom throughout the weekend.

Alternatively Bus to Tarragona

If you can't rent a car, take the bus from the airport to Tarragona. You can book online at busplana.com or buy your ticket directly at the airport.

Taxi from Tarragona to Altafulla

From the Tarragona bus or train station, take a taxi to your hotel in Altafulla. Radio Taxi Tarragona is the local taxi company.

Coming from Barcelona?

You can rent a car in Barcelona or take a train from the Barcelona-Sants station. You can take the local train: lines R15 or R17 to the station Altafulla-Tamarit or the high-speed rail: Renfe, Ouigo and Iryo to the station Camp de Tarragona and then take a taxi to your hotel in Altafulla.
